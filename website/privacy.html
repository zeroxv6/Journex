Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

Data Collection

Journex is designed with privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Google Drive Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable Google Drive sync, your journal entries and notes are synced to your personal Google Drive account. We do not have access to your Google Drive data. The sync feature uses Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication, and you can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings.

Analytics

Journex does not use any analytics, tracking, or telemetry services. Your usage patterns and data remain completely private.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service Journex interacts with is Google Drive (if you enable sync). No other external services are used.

Open Source

Journex is open source software. You can review the entire codebase at github.com/zeroxv6/Journex to verify our privacy claims.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
