Data Deletion Policy

Important: We Do Not Store Your Data

Journex does not store any of your personal data on our servers. All your journal entries, notes, tasks, and other information remain exclusively on your device and, if you choose to enable sync, on your personal Google Drive account.

How Your Data is Stored

Journex is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • Local Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device. We never transmit your personal information to our servers.
  • Optional Google Drive Sync: If you enable Google Drive sync, your data is stored in your personal Google Drive account, not on Journex servers.
  • No Third-Party Access: We do not have access to your Google Drive data. Only you control your information.
  • End-to-End Control: You have complete ownership and control over all your data at all times.

How to Delete Your Data

Since we don't store your data on our servers, deleting your information is entirely within your control. Follow these simple steps:

1

Delete Google Drive Sync Data (if enabled)

If you enabled Google Drive sync:

  • Open Google Drive
  • Locate the JournalAppBackup or JournalingAppMedia folder
  • Right-click the folder and select "Move to trash"
  • Empty your Google Drive trash to permanently delete the data
2

Revoke App Access to Google Drive

Remove Journex's access to your Google account:

  • Go to your Google Account Security settings
  • Navigate to "Third-party apps with account access"
  • Find "Journex" or "Journal App Desktop" in the list
  • Click "Remove Access"
3

Delete Local Data

Remove all data stored on your device:

  • Android: Uninstall the app from your device. This will delete all local data including journals, notes, tasks, and settings.
  • Desktop (Windows/Mac/Linux): Uninstall the application. All local data will be removed during uninstallation.

Additional Information

What Data is Deleted?

Following the steps above will permanently delete:

  • All journal entries and notes
  • Tasks, schedules, and reminders
  • Templates and prompts
  • Media files (photos, voice recordings, drawings)
  • App settings and preferences
  • All sync data from Google Drive

Data Retention

Since Journex does not store your data on our servers, there is no data retention period on our end. Once you delete your local data and Google Drive files, your information is permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
